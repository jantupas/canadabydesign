Winter Snow Train
to the Canadian Rockies


Book Vacation Now

On our best selling Canadian Rockies winter train trip, journey through the winter scenery of Banff, Jasper and Lake Louise with a night in Sleeper Plus Class on VIA Rail. Spend days in the Canadian Rockies enjoying Ice Walks, wildlife viewing, dog sledding and sleigh rides, and nights tucking into cozy Canadian mountain resorts. In Jasper, we've included your choice of three tours, and in Banff you'll have an entire day at leisure to do as you please. Customize it to include snowshoe helicopter tours, ice climbing and more!

Book Now


Itinerary Overview

Day 1 - Vancouver to Jasper | Via Rail

Day 2 - Jasper | Arrival Via Rail

Day 3 - Jasper Freedom of Choice | 1 of 4 Excursions

  1. Maligne Canyon Ice Walk
  2. Discover Jasper & its Wildlife Tour
  3. Jasper Dark Sky Planetarium & Telescope Experience
  4. Upgrade Option: Dog Sledding Tour

Day 4 - Jasper to Lake Louise | Coach | Evening Sleigh Ride

Day 5 - Lake Louise to Banff | Coach

Day 6 - Banff Freedom of Choice | 1 of 4 Excursions

  1. Discover Banff & Its Wildlife Tour
  2. Snowshoeing at Paint Pots
  3. Johnston Canyon Icewalk
  4. Banff Gondola

Day 7 - Banff to Calgary | Coach
