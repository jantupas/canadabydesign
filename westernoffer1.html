Canadian Rockies & the White Pass
Yukon Railroads


Book Vacation Now

This one-of-a-kind train journey takes you on the VIA Rail to the Canadian Rockies and the White Pass Yukon Railroad. With our easy-to-customize freedom of choice tours in both Jasper and Banff, you can make this itinerary entirely your own with wildlife viewing, a Maligne Valley tour, the Banff Gondola, or a cowboy cookout, you'll experience Jasper, Lake Louise, and Banff, as well as the northern cities of Whitehorse and Skagway, on "The Scenic Railway of the World."

Book Now


Itinerary Overview

Day 1 - Vancouver to Jasper | Via Rail

Day 2 - Arrive Jasper | Via Rail

Day 3 - Jasper | Freedom of Choice - Choose 1 of 2 Excursions

  1. Morning Maligne Valley with Medicine Lake Tour
  2. Evening Discover Jasper & its Wildlife Tour

Day 4 - Jasper to Lake Louse | Sightseeing with picnic lunch

  • Ice Explorer Ride onto the Athabasca Glacier + Glacier Skywalk

Day 5 - Lake Louise to Banff | Coach

Day 6 - Banff | Freedom of Choice - Choose 1 of 3 Excursions

  1. Discover Banff & Its Wildlife Tour + Banff Gondola
  2. Discover the Grizzly Bears Lake & Waterfalls
  3. Cowboy Horseback Cookout BBQ Lunch or Dinner

Day 7 - Banff to Whitehorse | Coach + Flight

Day 8 - Whitehorse | City Tour

Day 9 - Whitehorse to Skagway Excursion | White Pass & Yukon Railroad

Day 10 - Whitehorse to Vancouver | Flight
