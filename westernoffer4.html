Discover Whistler and the Canadian Rockies
Road Trip


Book Vacation Now

On our Discover Whistler and the Canadian Rockies Road Trip, you'll get a taste of Western Canada. This itinerary, which includes the Whistler Peak 2 Peak Gondola, an Ice Explorer Ride on the Athabasca Glacier, and your choice of tours in Jasper and Banff, is great for people who like to travel at a leisurely pace. Everything has been meticulously prepared, including the greatest Canadian Rockies hotels and unusual activities. Plus, with our special rates, you may pick up your rental car in Vancouver and drop it off in Calgary without paying a one-way fee!

Book Now


Itinerary Overview

Day 1 - Vancouver to Whistler

Day 2 - Whistler | Peak 2 Peak Gondola Ride

Day 3 - Whistler to Sun Peak

Day 4 - Sun Peaks to Jasper

Day 5 - Jasper | Freedom of Choice - Choose 1 of 3 Excursions

  1. Maligne Lake Cruise
  2. Jasper River Float
  3. Jasper Nature Walk

Day 6 - Jasper to Banff | Ice Explorer + Skywalk

  • Ice Explorer Ride onto the Athabasca Glacier + Glacier Skywalk

Day 7 - Banff | Freedom of Choice - Choose 1 of 4 Excursions

  1. Banff Gondola
  2. Lake Minnewanka Cruise
  3. River Explorer Canoe Tour
  4. Bow River Horseback Adventure

Day 8 - Banff to Calgary
