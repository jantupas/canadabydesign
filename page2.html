We Are

Canada By Design



We are a Canadian travel agency offering our clients rewarding experiences by curating each holiday to suit your travel style. Dreaming of a vacation in Canada? We like you already. As Canadian vacation experts backed by over 20 years of experience, we take the hassle out of trip planning by delivering complete Canadian vacations personalized to your preferences and based on first-hand experience. Covering the best of Canada from coast to coast, our long-standing partnerships guarantee quality experiences at competitive rates.

Our mission is to use the experience of the most seasoned travel professionals, as well as their industry alliances, to provide greater value and service, as well as a wider range of elegant and experiential vacations, to inspire and meet the demands of all of our clients. Our network connects us to the greatest travel suppliers and partners all around the world as proud members of Discover Holidays. As a result, we are able to provide you with special discounts and VIP offers.

By combining a global network, custom-crafted itineraries, and the finest level of service, our objective is to provide extraordinary value to all travelers seeking fresh Canada travel and vacation experiences.



A Personalized

Travel Experience


We have full-service Canada by Design travel agents and agencies positioned around the country to assist you in making the most of your next leisure or business trip.

Our agents will spend time getting to know your specific travel interests and goals until we "get you," and then we'll use our education, first-hand travel experience, and relationships with airlines, car, hotel, resort, and tour partners to ensure you have an unforgettable personalized travel experience.

We act as your personal travel advocate, assisting you with everything from getting you on the next available flight if yours is canceled to researching all of your options to locate you the perfect vacation package, rail, excursions, restaurants, and more. Only a skilled and experienced travel professional can provide you with the suggestions, recommendations, and insights that we can. We live and breathe travel, and we'd love to show you how we can make your journeys more enjoyable.

Agents with Canada by Design know where you want to travel, how to get there, and what priceless experiences you want to enjoy. We know who you are and how to get you there.



We Ensure

Prestige & Security

Collaborate directly with your specialized consultant, who will work with you one-on-one to learn about your vacation preferences. Your consultant will create bespoke itineraries for an amazing travel experience from planning to booking.

There are no booking costs. Mastercard and Visa have no credit card fees. Checking visas and passports is safe and secure. Complete assistance with all elements of your vacation preparations

All funds received by us are held in a Trust Account, and payments from this account are used solely to fund our customers' vacation arrangements. Our debt-free status is something we are quite proud of. We have an independent accredited auditor perform our annual financial audit.
