Central Canada

Travel To The Center


Central Canada vacations take you through prairie grasslands, lakes, woodland, and the sub-Arctic tundra, and are made comprised of Saskatchewan and Manitoba. Churchill, Manitoba, has the highest number of polar bears in Canada, as well as some of the best wildlife viewing opportunities. If you have any questions, please contact our specialists, who have personally experienced these journeys.
