Why Choose

Canada By Design?

We understand tailor made travel – your expectations will not only be met on one of our holidays, but exceeded every time. We truly pride ourselves on our commitment to travel planning. We carefully design trips from the traveler’s perspective - paying close attention to logistics, quality and comfort to deliver unique experiences across all areas of Canada. Our trips come with expert insight and personal recommendations during the planning process and ensure you have a trusted partner working in the background while you travel – so you can just sit back, relax and enjoy the journey.

Our travel designers have traveled extensively throughout Canada, Alaska and the South Pacific and can help create the perfect vacation. Our company has been established and travelled for over 20 years. Our Travel Designers are not only experienced globe-trotters, they regularly travel throughout Canada to gain first-hand knowledge of what we offer.
